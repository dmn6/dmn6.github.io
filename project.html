1 Introduction

Our project matters because studying average global IQ scores per country will give insight into the cognitive ability of populations around the world. This information can help researchers and policymakers understand the distribution of intellectual abilities on a global scale.

Intelligence is often linked to economic productivity and social well-being. Analyzing IQ data can help researchers explore the relationship between average IQ scores and economic indicators, such as GDP per capita, employment rates, and innovation.

Our project is about bringing awareness to the educational system and how it needs to be improved globally.

# Introduction

Our project matters because studying average global IQ scores per country will give insight into the cognitive ability of populations around the world. This information can help researchers and policymakers understand the distribution of intellectual abilities on a global scale.

Intelligence is often linked to economic productivity and social well-being. Analyzing IQ data can help researchers explore the relationship between average IQ scores and economic indicators, such as GDP per capita, employment rates, and innovation.

Our project is about bringing awareness to the educational system and how it needs to be improved globally.
